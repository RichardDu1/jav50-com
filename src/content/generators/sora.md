## Motion Coherence & Realism
Sora's motion coherence is **industry-leading**. Objects move with natural physics, and camera pans, zooms, and subject tracking are smooth without jarring jumps. Temporal stability over 10-60 second clips is remarkable, with minimal flickering or texture swimming. Realism is **photorealistic** in many outputs, though occasional uncanny valley effects appear in human faces and hands.

## Prompt Adherence
Sora excels at interpreting complex prompts, including multiple subjects, actions, and environmental details. It correctly handles spatial relationships (e.g., "a cat on the left, a dog on the right") and stylistic requests (e.g., "in the style of a vintage film"). However, it may still struggle with precise counts or very abstract concepts.

## Artifacts & Limitations
While vastly superior to competitors, Sora is not flawless. Common artifacts include **morphing of small objects**, **inconsistent lighting across cuts**, and occasional **body part disconnection** in humans. The model also has difficulty with rapid motion or complex interactions (e.g., two people shaking hands).

## The Verdict
Sora is a **game-changer** for AI video generation, setting a new benchmark for quality and coherence. Its ability to produce long, stable, and realistic videos from text prompts is unmatched. However, limited public access and high computational cost mean it's currently out of reach for most creators. For those who can use it, Sora is the **gold standard**.
